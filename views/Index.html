OUR CORE SKILLS

Language Skills

Development is from words to phrases to sentences. Stories, puppet play, rhymes, concept learning, art, role play, music; every activity develops language and communication skills.

Gross Motor Skills

Ample opportunities to develop gross motor skills related to large muscles - running, jumping, hopping. Montessori activities, EPL activities, free play, physical activities...

Social/Emotional Skills

One of the most important skills to develop in early childhood which lays the foundation for your habits/values in the future.

Cognitive Skills

Cognitive functions are developed by activities which involve listening, understanding and paying attention.

OUR JOURNEY

Munchkin Foundation

2019-2022

Munchkin Preschool is a leading chain of preschools located in India and Nepal backed by Zee Learn Ltd. With more than two decades of experience in the preschool industry, Kidzee has nurtured more than 1.5 million children throughout India.

Curriculum Development

2022-2023

Our curriculum is meticulously designed, integrating modern pedagogical approaches with traditional values, focusing on holistic child development across cognitive, social, emotional, and physical domains.

Community Engagement

2023-Present

Active involvement with parents and the local community ensures a supportive and enriching environment for our students. Regular workshops and events foster a strong school-home partnership.

Infrastructure Expansion

2024

We are continuously investing in state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, child-friendly playgrounds, and innovative learning tools, to provide the best possible learning environment.

Teacher Training & Development

Ongoing

Our dedicated educators receive continuous training in the latest teaching methodologies and child psychology, ensuring they are equipped to provide exceptional guidance and care.

Digital Learning Integration

2025

Embracing technology, we've integrated digital learning tools and interactive platforms into our curriculum, preparing children for the modern world while keeping learning fun and engaging.

Health & Wellness Programs

Ongoing

Prioritizing the well-being of our students, we've launched comprehensive health and wellness programs, including balanced nutrition plans and regular physical activity sessions.

Future Vision

2025 Onwards

We envision expanding our reach, introducing more specialized programs, and continually evolving our approach to early childhood education to nurture future leaders and innovators.
